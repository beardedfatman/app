We care

From the selection of excellent ingredients, to the meticulous execution of our fine-tuned recipes; from the the packing of our pastries, to the timeliness of our deliveries - We aim to give you the best possible experience. All of this, a small price to pay for that smile on your face!

Our story

In 2011 Anne, driven by her passion for making pastries, started off baking from humble beginnings at the age of 9. Her main learning resources: baking books and recipes found online. Her baked goods shared solely with her loved ones: family and friends.

Today After attending a multitude of courses on pastry product and packaging, Anne, with the same love but a nurtured confidence, hopes to bring her delicious cakes to all of you in the Serangoon vicinity!

Our System

As of early 2018, Anne's Bakery operates as a home bakery with delivery/pick-up services; from Serangoon, to all of you, in Serangoon.

Upon submission of your order, our Bakery kicks into action and prepares the cakes with loving passion. Once completed, your order is packaged carefully and stored in the most optimal of conditions. The cakes are passed over to Anne's cautious Deliverymen, and your pastries, excited to delight your taste buds, are handed over to you. For those of you who prefer, we have a pick-up option available too!
